Short Bio

Dr. Yushuai Li is an assistant professor in Department of Computer Science at Aalborg University. He has published more than 70 papers in prestigious journals and conferences. Two of them are “Hot Papers and eight of them are "Highly Cited Papers". He has an h-index of 24 and 2500+ citations according to Google scholar. He received the Best Paper Awards from Journal of Modern Power Systems and Clean Energy (MPCE), 2023 International Conference on Cyber-energy Systems and Intelligent Energies (ICCSIE), and 2024 IEEE Conference on Energy Internet and Energy System Integration (EI2). He received the Excellent Young Expert Award from MPCE (2023), the CAA Second Prize of National Natural Science (2022), the CAA First Prize of National Natural Science (2023). He served as the Student Video / PHD Thesis Competition Chair for IEEE SmartGridComm 2024, Section Chair for SPIES 2022 and IEEE EI2 2022, and Track Chair for ISIE 2024. He is currently the associate editors of IEEE Transactions on Industrial Informatics, IEEE Transactions on Automation Science and Engineering, IEEE Systems, Man, and Cybernetics Magazine, and Journal of Modern Power Systems and Clean Energy.

Research Interests

My primary research is cooperative and digital operation for integrated energy and transportation systems. Specifically, my current research interests are as follows:

  • Reinforcement learning
  • Deep learning
  • Digital twin
  • Energy Internet (integrated energy and transportation systems)
  • Autonomous driving
