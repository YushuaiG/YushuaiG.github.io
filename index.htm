Short Bio

Dr. Yushuai Li is a Marie Curie Postdoctoral Fellow in Department of Informatics at University of Oslo. He received the Ph.D. degree in control theory and control engineering from Northeastern University, China in 2019. From 2019 to 2020, she worked as a Postdoc in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of Denver, Colorado, USA. From 2020 to 2022, he worked as a lecture in College of Information Science and Engibeering, Northeastern University, China. He joined University of Oslo in January 2022.

Research Interests

My primary research is cooperative and digital operation for integrated energy and transportation systems. Specifically, my current research interests are as follows:

  • Distributed optimization
  • Machine learning
  • Digital twin
  • Energy Internet (integrated energy and transportation systems)
  • Autonomous driving
